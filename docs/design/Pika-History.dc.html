Shang Dynasty, China c. 1600–1046 BCE
#ancient-china #bronze-age #writing
boltPINNED · ASIA · 1200 BCE editEdit

Oracle bones & the first Chinese writing

The Shang ruled the Yellow River valley and left the earliest confirmed Chinese script — carved into ox scapulae and turtle plastrons for divination. Diviners applied heat, then read the cracks as ancestral answers.

  • Kings consulted ancestors about harvests, war, and weather
  • ~150,000 inscribed fragments catalogued, mostly from Anyang
  • Script is a direct ancestor of modern Chinese characters
"To the Shang, the past was never gone — the ancestors sat on the council."
